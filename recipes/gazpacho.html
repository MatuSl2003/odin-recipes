Spanish Gazpacho

Description

Spanish gazpacho is easy to make, super healthy, bursting with flavor, and absolutely delicious! It's usually served as an appetizer in a small bowl with toppings, or from a glass as a drink. Either way, it really hits the spot on a hot day!

Steps

STEP 1 Theres only one step involved! Just mix cucumber, tomato, vinegar, onion, olive oil, pepper and croutons in a mixer.

Ingredients

  • 3 Fresh red Tomato
  • 1 medium-sized cucumber
  • 3 spoon-fulls of olive oil
  • 1 medium-sized onion
  • Croutons (about 15-20 would suffice)
  • 1 large Pepper (either red or green)
  • 1 spoon-full of vinegar
