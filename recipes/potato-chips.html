Potato Chips

Description

Homemade salted potato chips! Crunchy, cheap and may I say, addictive as f***.

Steps

STEP 1 Peel and wash your potatoes

STEP 2 Cut them with a slicer, so as to make them thin-ish

STEP 3 Put oil on a tray, either olive or sunflower (not car one), and place the potato chips with some salt seasoning, you may add extra seasoning if you wish

STEP 4 Cook them for about 10-15 mintes at medium heat and check them for crunchyness, not too little nor too much

Ingredients

  • 4 medium-sized Potatoes
  • Salt and seasoning (your choice)
  • Olive or Sunflower oil
